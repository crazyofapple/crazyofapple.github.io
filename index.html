Biography

I am currently a fourth-year PhD student at Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen), advised by Prof. Qingcai Chen and Assoc. Prof. Baotian Hu. I was a research intern at Tencent (3 times, 2017 MIG, 2018 TEG, 2019 CDG), and have been recommended as a member of CIPS Youth Working Committee 2019-2020 Student Committee. I also was funded by Tencent Rhino Bird Elite Talents in 2018, National Scholarship in 2018. I won 1st place in the CCKS 2019 financial entity extraction task and 1st place in the EMNLP 2019 BioNLP-OST AGAC track task evaluation. My current research interests focus on information extraction, NLP reasoning, medical & financial NLP, and model robustness & interpretability.
