Biography

I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen) under the guidance of Prof. Min Zhang and Prof. Aiguo Wu. I was a research intern at Tencent (3 times, 2017 MIG, 2018 TEG, 2019 CDG), and have been recommended as a member of CIPS Youth Working Committee 2019-2020 Student Committee. I also was funded by Tencent Rhino Bird Elite Talents in 2018, National Scholarship in 2018. I won 1st place in the CCKS 2019 financial entity extraction task and 1st place in the EMNLP 2019 BioNLP-OST AGAC track task evaluation. My current research interests focus on Natural Language Processing, Interpretability, Large Language Models, Trustworthy ML.
